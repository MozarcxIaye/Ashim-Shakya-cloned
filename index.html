About.

            As a person who's very aware of his surroundings, Bonish Shrestha spills into his canvas what he absorbs from the environment and the society. He experiments with various visual and audible mediums to unleash his sub-conscious to the physical dimension. As an expert on fields like calligraphy, digital / traditional painting, cinematography and direction, music production and a 3D generalist, he has worked with various clients worldwide and his artworks has been recognized globally.
